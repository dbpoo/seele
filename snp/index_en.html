What is SeeleN Industrial Autonomous Ecology？

SeeleN Industrial Ssset Value Exchange Platform introduces the decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) model to form an "industrial autonomous ecology" for the platform.

The formation of the industrial autonomous ecology not only benefits the open governance of industrial communities, but also promotes excellent industrial resources to enter SeeleN platform, so as to benefit users and form a virtuous circle.

With the aid of the distributed governance ecosystem architecture, it can be ensured that super nodes have fully decentralized voting rights and income rights, and conduct community governance openly and transparently.

The Origin of Community Governance Token SNP

Needed by Community Governance

In order to realize the good operation of the industrial autonomous ecology, the community governance token SNP is issued, and it forms SeeleN's dual token ecology with the value coin SEELE.

Create More Value

SNP has the right to participate in the governance of SeeleN and Nerv Ledger asset management platforms, and it can also share the development dividends of the dual platforms.

Mining Mechanism of SNP

Introduction of the mining pool:there are 1.53 million SNPs in the mining pool in total, which will be mined for 5 years, with an average daily output of 805.479452 and an annual output of 306,000.

Mining mechanism: Stake SEELE to mine SNP，daily settlement and distribution, according to the amount of staking and staking cycle, calculate the proportion of weight of the day, and allocate SNP according to the weight.

Detailed explanation for the revenue of community governance token SNP
SNP is a DAO governance token with dual value. Nerv Ledger issues SNP as Dao governance token, and SNP is also the Dao governance token for SeeleN's whole ecology.

Revenue Sharing

The profits generated by commissions, handling fees and management fees of all products on Nerv Ledger asset management platform will be repurchased in the secondary market of SNP. Share the benefits of SNP ecological growth with various mobile staking users and contributors in the system.

Mining Revenue

Holding SNP can participate in liquidity mining in Nerv Ledger system, and it can also participate in staking mining after SeeleN mainnet launched, which is an extremely valuable investment.

Voting Governance

Both Nerv Ledger and SeeleN need a large user group to participate and build together. A free, open, and transparent financial world; a fast, safe, and industry-supporting blockchain platform requires SNP holders to jointly govern and determine the growth and development of the system. SNP can conduct DAO voting on Nerv Ledger platform to govern its product development and govern SeeleN blockchain through staking.
